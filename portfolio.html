Colorado Symphony Poster Mailer


This is a project for my typography class. I had to take 12 seperate Colorado Symphony concerts and organize them onto a poster mailer. The poster was aimed at combining expressive and informational design using a grid. Additionally it had to be able to be folded and theoretically mailed, while not distorting the text on the poster.

Type Specimen Poster:


This type specimen poster is a piece of design that demonstrates the range of a typeface. It is also composed entirely of the typeface Superclarendon, requiring abstraction of the letterforms to create an aesthetically interesting composition. I also added some history of the typeface for fun.
